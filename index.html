About Olive Beach

Olive Beach is a Mediterranean-inspired restaurant in Bangalore, known for its charming ambiance,which resembles a Greek villa, and gourmet food. It offers dishes like the 8-hour lamb and 10-minute polenta, has a wine and cocktail list. It is popular for events like Sunday brunch and flea nights.
